Littleton, Colorado

Location

Historic downtown Littleton is located approximately 9 miles south of downtown Denver.

About

Elevation

When you come to Colorado, one thing you'll notice is that the green signs displaying the name of the town or city you're entering doesn't include the population number. Instead, you'll see the location's elevation. The elevation as listed in wikipedia is 5,351 feet above sea level, although the elevation where my house is located is almost 500 feet higher.

Population

According to 2020 census numbers, the population of Littleton is 45,652. But, with all the traffic around here, that number seems really low to me!

Weather

According to best places, On average, there are 246 sunny days per year in Littleton. The US average is 205 sunny days. Littleton averages 69 inches of snow per year. The US average is 28 inches of snow per year. But, don't worry, because as one funny meme went, "Welcome to Colorado where the where the weather is made up and the seasons don't matter!".
